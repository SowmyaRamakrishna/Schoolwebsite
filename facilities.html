Our Facilities

The school offers a variety of facilities to support student learning and well-being. The library is well-equipped to foster a love for reading, research, and academic exploration. Students also have access to advanced computer labs with updated technology to improve their digital literacy and technical skills. For hands-on learning, the science lab provides an interactive space where students can perform experiments and better understand scientific concepts. To ensure the health and well-being of every student, regular health check-ups are conducted, and nutritious mid-day meals are provided daily to support both physical health and academic performance.
