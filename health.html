Health Check-Ups for Students

Regular health check-ups are conducted to monitor the well-being of students. This School plays a critical role in helping students establish lifelong bealthy behaviours. Recognizing the importance of this, school based health promotion activities have been incorporated as a part of the Health and Wellness component of the Balayesu Vidyalaya Programme School Health & Wellness Programme every year is being implemented in government and government unaided schools. Two teachers, preferably one male and one female, in our school, designated as "Health and Well- nesa Ambassadors" shall be trained to transact with school children, health promotion and disease prevention. In these areas in the form of interesting joyful interactive activities for one hour every week.
