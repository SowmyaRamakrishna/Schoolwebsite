Admissions

  • Open Admission Policy: Balayesu School is a co-educational institution that provides admission to children from all communities, regardless of caste, creed, religion, or region, in accordance with its admission guidelines.
  • Admission Schedule: The admission process at Balayesu School usually commences in the first week of November every year.
  • Fair Admission Criteria: Admissions are offered solely on the basis of seat availability and not influenced by any discriminatory factors such as caste, creed, or religion.
  • Seat Availability for Classes: A limited number of seats are available for Nursery, LKG, and UKG, while admissions to Classes I to X depend on the availability of seats, which is often minimal.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Interview and Proficiency Test: Parents seeking admission for their children at Balayesu School must ensure the child appears for an interview and a proficiency test.
  • Syllabus-Based Testing: Students applying for admission may be evaluated based on the syllabus of the class immediately below the one they are applying for.
  • Age Criteria for Nursery: Children seeking admission to Nursery must have completed 3 years of age as of 1st June of the admission year.
  • Age Criteria for Class 1: Children applying for Class 1 must have completed 6 years of age as of 1st June of the admission year.
  • Age Criteria for Other Classes: The age requirement for admission to other classes will follow the same guidelines, based on 1st June of the admission year.
  • Document Verification: Parents must ensure that all necessary documents, including proof of age, are submitted during the admission process.
  • Seat Confirmation: Admission will be confirmed only after successful completion of the interview, proficiency test, and document verification, subject to seat availability.
  • Admission Form: Parents must fill out the Admission Form in English and submit it to the school office for the academic year 2025-26.
